Welcome to Student Explorer!

Student Explorer helps academic advisors identify academically at-risk students using Learning Management System data.

Search for any student

Type the student's name, uniqname, or UMID in the search bar above to explore their academic performance. You can find any U-M Ann Arbor student, including graduate students, in Student Explorer.

Create your student list

Want to see a list of your students all in one place? Student Explorer lets you view a list of your students, called a "cohort," for quick and easy reference. Learn more about cohorts and how to set one up.

Learn more

Visit our About page to learn more about the purpose and history of Student Explorer, or visit Resources & Support for information on how to use Student Explorer.
